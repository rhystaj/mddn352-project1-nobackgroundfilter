Locals make the best guides

Want a day out, but don't know where to start? Create a profile specifying your interests and request a day out, tailored to your requirements. Then, let someone who feels they know the area well guide you to places you may have never known about.

Give them live feedback, so they adjust the day even further to accommodate you.

Put your knowledge to the test

How well do you really know a city? Answer a request from other users for an outing and plan a series of activities for them based on thier specifications.

Browse events in the area, suggest activities to the assigned users, track thier location in real-time and recieve feedback as they complete your suggested activities. Data such as budget and time will be tracked to help you stay within the specifications.

The user feedback will be accumulated into a final score.
